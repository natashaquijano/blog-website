A little more about us


Mine is about sharing and curating a thoughtful space where humans can appreciate the simplicity of life while being able to take something with them and constantly be reminded that a decluttered life is a decluttered mind.

Our mission is to make this world a better place and help our customers become the best versions of themselves by living a simplistic life. We truly believe that our store layout and members of our staff guide consumers to buy only what they really need.

We try to position our brand as a wellness journey instead of a clothing and accessories boutique.
